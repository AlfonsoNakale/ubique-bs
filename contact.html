Frequently asked questions.

Answers to questions you might have about UBS Tourism CRM.

What makes our CRM solution different from other hospitality CRMs?

Our solution combines Zoho's powerful integrated ecosystem with hospitality-specific features, offering seamless management of guest relationships, bookings, and operations. Unlike standalone CRMs, our solution provides end-to-end integration across all business functions.

Is the system suitable for both small and large properties?

Yes! Our solution is scalable and customizable to fit properties of all sizes. Whether you're managing a single property or multiple destinations, the system can be configured to meet your specific needs.

How long does it take to implement the system?

Typical implementation takes 2-4 weeks, depending on your requirements and data migration needs. Our team provides dedicated support throughout the process to ensure a smooth transition.

How long does it take to learn the system?

Basic functions can be learned in 1-2 days. Full proficiency typically takes 2-3 weeks, depending on user roles and system utilization.

Can I migrate data from my existing CRM system?

Yes, we offer comprehensive data migration services from most major CRM platforms. Our team ensures your historical guest data, bookings, and other important information are transferred securely.

Does the system integrate with existing Property Management Systems (PMS)?

Yes, our solution integrates with major PMS platforms, ensuring seamless data flow between systems. We can provide a list of supported integrations during consultation.

Can I customize the system to match my brand and workflow?

Absolutely! The system offers extensive customization options, including branded templates, custom fields, automated workflows, and personalised dashboards.

What pricing plans are available?

We offer flexible pricing plans based on your business size and needs. Contact our sales team for a customised quote that fits your requirements.
